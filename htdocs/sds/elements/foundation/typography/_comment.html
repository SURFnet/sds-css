Styling is applied to generic tags: h1 - h6, p, a, ul and ol. Note that margin is removed from these elements. They recieve basic spacing when inside .text--rich.

The class.text--rich also applies the correct palette color and other styling to a link(a).

Heading styling can also be applied via classes (eg .text--h2). Common text styling are available as classes(.text--body, .text--body--small and .text--body--large).
